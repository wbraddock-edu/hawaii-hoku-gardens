Kauai's Garden Experts

We Know This Island's
Gardens Like Home

Hoku Gardens was founded by Haunani and Keoni Perez, siblings who grew up on their parents' farm in Anahola. They've spent their lives working the unique volcanic soils of Kauai's North Shore and East Side, developing an intimate knowledge of what grows here and why.

"Kauai is the wettest island. The soil, the humidity, the wind patterns, the salt exposure near the coast — it all matters. We design landscapes that work with the island, not against it."
