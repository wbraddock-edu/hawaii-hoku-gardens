Founded 2017

Rooted in Kauai's Soil

Hoku Gardens was founded by siblings Haunani and Keoni Perez, who grew up working on their parents' taro farm in Anahola. They've been around Kauai's unique volcanic red soil their entire lives, understanding what flourishes here better than any mainland-trained landscaper ever could.

Haunani holds a degree in landscape architecture from UH Mānoa, where she specialized in native Hawaiian plant ecosystems. Keoni is a certified arborist and irrigation specialist with 15+ years of hands-on experience across Kauai's North Shore, East Side, and South Shore.

"We started Hoku Gardens because we were frustrated with mainland landscaping companies coming to Kauai with one-size-fits-all solutions that ignored the island's ecology. Every place on Kauai is different. We design for the actual place — the elevation, the rainfall, the wind exposure, the soil type."
